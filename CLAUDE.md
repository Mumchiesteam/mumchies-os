\# Mumchies OS - Claude Instructions



\## Project overview



Mumchies OS is an internal operations platform for Mumchies Foods.



The objective is to consolidate and automate daily ecommerce operations across:



\- Shopify

\- Shiprocket

\- Shadowfax

\- Delhivery

\- Internal order operations

\- Courier booking

\- Shipment tracking

\- NDR handling

\- Shipping label generation

\- Shopify fulfilment updates



The primary user is a non-technical business operator. Changes must prioritise reliability, simplicity, and operational safety.
